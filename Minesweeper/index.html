Select the size of grid you want to play in and press the play button.

Left click on cells to reveal their contents but
be careful with mines, since once you find one it's game over.

In order to win you need to flag each of the mines, and
to do that right click on the cell that possibly could have a mine.

You only have limited flags so use them wisely; however, you can unflag a cell
to recover one flag by right clicking on a flagged cell.

Try to score as less as you can since the number of clicks is your score, a lower score,
less clicks took the player to win.
